    Our Mission

    TAM Foundation is dedicated to transforming the lives of poor and orphaned children in Uganda by providing access to quality education, nutritious food, safe shelter, and comprehensive healthcare. We believe every child deserves a chance to dream, grow, and succeed.

    What we Do

    Our comprehensive programs support children at every stage

    Education Support

    Providing school fees, uniforms, and learning materials to children in need.

    Nutrition Program

    Ensuring children receive nutritious meals to support healthy growth and development.

    Shelter & Care

    Safe housing and loving care for orphaned and vulnerable children.

    Healthcare

    Access to medical care, vaccinations, and health monitoring for all children.

    Stories of Hope

    Molly's Journey

    After losing her parents, 8-year-old Molly found a new home with TAM Foundation. Today, she's excelling in school and dreams of becoming a teacher to help other children like herself.

    Megan's Struggles

    In a dusty Ugandan village, orphaned Meghan sketched in the dirt, dreaming beyond poverty. When a local artist discovered her talent, she traded charcoal for real hope. No longer just a village girl, Meghan’s art became her ticket to a world where she finally belonged.
